Monograph

The book about 'Gemme d'arti italiane' (in Italian) that this site complements sets this periodical against the cultural background of its time and follows an informative and critical path through its 14 volumes and 176 works of art.

The book is available in hard copy on Amazon.

A text-only version of the monograph (hypertext in PDF format) can also be downloaded for free here.
